How to prevent a fire!

1. Don't play with fire

Playing with fire is one of the common things that can make a fire especially if you're at a wooden house as it can destroy the house within seconds. This is why parents must always guard their kids when touching flammable as not only it can hurt their kid but also them selves.

2. Always turn off your stove or gas

Every parent tells us this, everytime they had to go somewhere they always remind us to turn off the gas as there could be a chance that it could leak and start a fire. Also parents must fix if there are any leaks to reduce the chances of exploding.

3. Don't use broken batteries

Always don't use broken batteries as there is a chance that they will explode without you knowing. Just like when it's near an electric circuit or when it's facing the sun, it can explode and make a fire.

4. Always check your wires

Some wires have expose sides or a broken part which when plugged, it can explode and cause a fire. This is why always be careful when plugging a wire with an exposed side.

5. Always have a fire extinguisher near you

Handling a fire without any fire extinguiser is hard, so make sure always have one near you to further erase the fire. When you have no fire extinguiser try using water as it can also help getting rid of the fire but the most important thing is having a fire extinguiser within your house.
